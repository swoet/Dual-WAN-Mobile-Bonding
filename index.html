Dual-WAN Mobile Bonding

An Android app that intelligently bonds Wi-Fi and mobile data simultaneously using a VPN service — doubling your effective bandwidth and providing seamless failover.

Bandwidth Aggregation

Routes traffic across both connections simultaneously for maximum throughput

Smart Failover

Instantly switches to a backup connection if the primary drops

Per-App Routing

Selectively route specific apps over each connection for fine-grained control
